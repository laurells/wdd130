Why choose contribution?

The Contribution is a safe and secure mobile platform for Peer-to-Peer funding. You can join contribution with other like-minded people from all over Nigeria and start saving or investing money here. In times of need, you can also borrow money which is multiples of what you have invested. Contribution provides you a group of verified members with whom you can pool up money.
