How Does The Contribution work?

Contribution is basically an investment option as well as a borrowing tool. In this type of savings, individuals come together and agree to pool-in a specific amount of money each month for a set period of time according to the number of persons in the group. At the end of the month the money made out of the contribution would be given out to the selected participant. The organizer makes sure that every participant is placed accordingly as agreed by the group as to how the mone would be given each month/week. The money contributed is given to each group member until the cycle finishes. It's simple and easy. Invest in The Contribution and make your saving, investing and borrowing money easier.
